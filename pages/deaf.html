TO EACH THEIR OWN

Short movie, 2019


What does it mean to be 9 and deaf?


This short film aims to explore different ways to experience the world, and tries find ways to translate it into solutions that let the audience identify with these different perceptions of reality.


The short eas shot using only an Iphone 6S as equipment.
